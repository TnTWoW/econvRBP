Before your submission.

In order for our website to correctly return results according to the files you submitted, please follow the steps below to process your data:

(1)Remove non-essential amino acids such as' X', 'Z' and 'B',etc.

(2)The head and sequence are on one line each.

(3)Upload the file and wait for a minute.

Result.

If you submit the correct sequence, we will return the following result:

>|your portein ID|

"binding portein" or "non binding portein"
