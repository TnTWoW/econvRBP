What is the econvRBP?

econvRBP is a free webserver for biologist, which can provides a friendly and concise interface for researchers, and has guiding significance for experimental methods.

How do econvRBP predict RBPs?

You need to submit the ".fasta" file to server, and we will automatically extract the features from the submitted sequence and send them to the convolutional neural network-based predictor and return the results to you.

Caution

Please read our help carefully before you use the server, otherwise we cannot guarantee that the feedback to you is correct.
